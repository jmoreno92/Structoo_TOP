Fillet weld capacity (LRFD-style)

Nominal strength of a longitudinal fillet weld per AISC 360-22 J2.4: Rn = Fnw Awe with Fnw = 0.60 FEXX and effective throat 0.707 × leg size. Design strength uses your resistance factor φ (default 0.75). If weld length is omitted, only line-load capacity is shown.

Per side, along the joint. Leave blank for φRn per foot only.

Results

For sizing only—verify electrode, load combination, effective length, and connection geometry against AISC 360 and your project standard. φ = 0.75 is typical for welds in LRFD; use the factor required by your jurisdiction.
